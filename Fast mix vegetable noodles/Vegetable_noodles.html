Mixed egg noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup noodles of your choosing/li>
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed-vegetables
  • Green onioins
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • protein of your choosing- Chicken, Bacon or Beef

Instructions

  1. First take 1 cup of noodles to 2 cups of water .
  3. Once it boils,take the noodles out making sure its only 80% cooked
  4. finallly start preparing sauce in a cup- add 1.5tbsp of soy and oyster sauce, 1.5tsp of dark sauce
  5. Add all the noodles i nthe pan alogn with the sauce
  6. stir well in medium heat for 2 min
  7. lwt it simmer for 5 min
  8. Finally add some chooped green onions and its ready to serve!! enjoy!!
