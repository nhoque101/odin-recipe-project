Mixed Fried Rice

It has mix vegetable and good amount of proteins. It's inspired by chineese food as it includes various soy sauce. It can take between 15-25 min cook it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Jasmine rice/Basmati rice uncooked
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed-vegetables
  • Green onioins
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • protein of your choosing- Chicken, Bacon or Beef

Instructions

  1. First take 1 cup of rice of your choosing and wash by rinsing it.
  2. Put 1.5 cup of water and bring it to a boil
  3. Once it boils, close the lid and let it simmer for 18 min then set it aside for 30 min
  4. hit up a pan with some oil then add your seasoned protein (bacon). once its 805 done add two eggs with salt and pepper
  5. After stiring the mix add 1 cup of mixed vegetbale
  6. stir everything in medium heat for 2 min
  7. finallly start preparing sauce in a cup- add 1.5tbsp of soy and oyster sauce, 1.5tsp of dark sauce
  8. Add all the rice i nthe pan alogn with the sauce
  9. stir well in medium heat for 2 min
  10. lwt it simmer for 5 min
  11. Finally add some chooped green onions and its ready to serve!! enjoy!!
